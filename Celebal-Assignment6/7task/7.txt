Task: Configure health probes for pods.

‚úÖ Configure Health Probes for Pods in AKS (Liveness & Readiness Probes)
    Health probes are used in Kubernetes to check if your pod is:

    Ready to accept traffic (Readiness Probe)

    Healthy and should keep running (Liveness Probe)

üõ†Ô∏è Step 1: Create a YAML File for Health Probes
Create a file: nginx-probes.yaml
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  name: nginx-health
spec:
  containers:
  - name: nginx
    image: nginx
    ports:
    - containerPort: 80
    livenessProbe:
      httpGet:
        path: /
        port: 80
      initialDelaySeconds: 10
      periodSeconds: 5
    readinessProbe:
      httpGet:
        path: /
        port: 80
      initialDelaySeconds: 5
      periodSeconds: 5

üõ†Ô∏è Step 2: Apply the YAML to Your AKS Cluster
   kubectl apply -f nginx-probes.yaml
Check pod status:kubectl get pods

üîç Step 3: Describe and Monitor the Pod
